# Piano Samples Attribution

## FluidR3 GM Acoustic Grand Piano

The piano samples in this directory are extracted from the **FluidR3 GM** SoundFont.

- **License:** MIT License
- **Source:** [midi-js-soundfonts](https://github.com/gleitz/midi-js-soundfonts)
- **Original SoundFont:** FluidR3_GM by Frank Wen
- **Format:** MP3, 44.1kHz stereo, 128kbps
- **Notes included:** C2 (MIDI 36), C3 (48), C4 (60), C5 (72), C6 (84)

Used for octave-spaced piano playback with runtime pitch shifting (±6 semitones per sample).
